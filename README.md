Project Summary:-
This undertaking involves 5 tasks and is to develop essential digital, technical, and professional skills.
Task one graphic.
An infographic was made by using a designing tool in this task. The assessment is made based on the tool used, visual quality of the design, coverage of the topic and a short reflection write-up on the process and what was learnt.
Step 2 – Digital Portfolio.
Setting up the digital portfolio platforms and keeping them complete. This also involves creating a proper GitHub README, as well as a reflection regarding the process of building a professional online presence.
Preparing for coding interview.
Coding Challenges were completed on curated platforms in this task. A Google Form was also created which was assessed with the quality of the same along with a reflection of the learning. 
Proper email behavior is crucial
A professional email is written through this task.  Completeness of a checklist; completeness of a write-up based on a scenario which shows you know how to write an e-mail.
Cybercrime Case Study: Task 5.
The task is to investigate a cyber crime case. The evaluation will be based on the accuracy, level of detail, quality of checklist and a reflection that describes insights and understanding that you acknowledged

MODULE SUMMARIES:-
Module I: Spreadsheets Presentation Applications
The module aims at developing basic skills in presentation and spreadsheet tools. It teaches you to create impactful presentations through PowerPoint, Prezi and Canva.  Students learn the basic functions of MS Excel such as formulas, functions, charts, and more. The module improves skills to visually communicate and represent data.

Module 2: Collaborative Programming Platforms.
Students will learn about various tools for programming and collaboration. Collaborate with teammate using Google Docs, Google Sheets, and Google Forms in this lesson. Coding practice platforms like HackerRank, CodeChef, and CodeTantra are also covered. The objective is to promote problem-solving skills and collaborative learning.

Module 3: digital portfolio development.
In this module, students learn to construct and manage their digital presence. Making profiles on sites like LinkedIn, GitHub, and exploring Quora, Stack Overflow also comes under this list. The emphasis is placed on displaying, exhibiting, and showcasing skills, projects, and accomplishments.

Module 4: Proper Behaviors in Emails and Social Media.
This module teaches proper communication in a digital environment. The course includes writing business emails, using proper attitude, structure, and other email features like attachments and signature. It emphasizes the need to be responsible and professional behavior in the online space.

Module 5: Understanding Cybercrime and Online Security.
This module talks about cyber-crimes and staying safe on the internet. It has types of cyber fraud, case studies, and preventive measures, etc.
