# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist 
* Do’s
•	Think before you post – Ask yourself if your post could affect your reputation later. 
•	Keep your profiles secure – Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings. 
•	Be respectful online – Treat others the way you would in real life. 
•	Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading fake news or rumours. 
•	Build a positive digital presence – Share achievements, skills, and meaningful content. 

* Don’ts
•	Don’t overshare personal information – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or private details. 
•	Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech – It can harm both others and your own image. 
•	Don’t click on suspicious links – They could lead to scams or phishing attacks. 
•	Don’t post when emotional or angry – You might regret it later. 
•	Don’t copy or repost content without giving credit – Respect others’ work and intellectual property.
